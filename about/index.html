Startup

In the university, I really wanted to build something meaninful with my skills.
I then, build a startup! The Procura Tecnologia.
We were 11 people in the begining. We created eVita, an app to check medicine interactions. It had more than 1 million interactions between medicines in the database. I was repsonsable for the development of the iOS and Android apps.
I decided to take the responsability and learn how to build iOS apps in my Samsung notebook! It went very well. I learnt Objective-C, XCode, and all the OS X environment. I really loved this stack.
That was the time I got passionated about iOS development (even with Objective-C). The startup went really well! We were one of the first in Brazil to have an iOS app on the App Store. We got many offers from investors to sell our database. We also got offers to build other apps.
Amazing times!
